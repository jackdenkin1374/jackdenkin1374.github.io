Being a Member

To become a member at JAMS you would have pay $7 for 1 semester or $10 for the school year.

The money goes to funding the club's annual convention, KiraKiraCon, and cosplay corner.

Being a member will give some perks in interacting with the club.

  • Nominating and Voting
    • 7 shows for each school semester
    • 1 openslot before each club meeting
    • 3 ~ 4 movies at each movie night
    • Discord server icon every 2 months or so

  • A chance for a free reimbursed ticket to a nearby summer conventions, such as Animazement.
    • This is because of UNCC travel grant for cultural events.

  • Access to various channels in the club's Discord server
  • Requests for materials if you are a member of the Cosplay Corner

Becoming an Officer

Any member of JAMS can become an officer for the club.
There are several rules in becoming one and presenting oneself as a candidate for the roles.

  • You must be a current student of UNCC.
  • You must be a due paying member of the Japanese Anime and Manga Society.
  • In order to apply for president, you must have held a different officer position for a minimum of 1 year.
  • In order to apply for a position, we require you to have a Discord account in order to make contact between candidates and current officers easier as well as to be a part of the Officers channel should you be selected.

During the last few weeks of the spring semester, the club will start officer election. They will send out a form where candidates can fill in information about themselves and their qualities as well as read the duties that the role has.
In the last meeting, the candidates will present themselves and give a short speech then the club members will vote whichever candidate is fit for the role.
In recent years, there has been a sprase amount of candidates and usually all of them gets assigned to each role.

There are 5 officer roles in the club, 1 of which was made recently in the past year.

  • President
    • Represent JAMS at UNCC in dealing with the faculty, administration, and the student legislature of UNC Charlotte, and to call and lead any necessary executive meetings.

  • Vice President
    • Successor to the President in case they are unable do to their duties or not available.

  • Treasurer
    • Represent JAMS at UNCC in dealing with the bank institution and handle the club's finances, such as KiraKiraCon, Cosplay Corner, Member Dues and any other money related matters.

  • Secretary
    • Note recorder of details at meetings, particularly officer meetings, and handle emails pertaining to the club and the club's annual convention.
    • Can be non essential if there is a lack of candidates.

  • Public Relations
    • Primary representive of JAMS when promoting both the club and the annual convention.
    • Recently made and can be non essential if there is a lack of candidates.
