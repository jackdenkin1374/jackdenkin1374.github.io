Welcome to the Japanese Anime and Manga Society Archive!

The purpose of this site is to record past events that taken place in this club for several past years. And various details about the club current and past history.
JAMS is a University of North Carolina at Charlotte interest club that allow students and alumni to gather in apreciation of Japanese culture.

The club meets every Wednesday from 6:30PM tp 11:00PM to watch 7 voted shows + 1 open slot that is voted by members during the meeting.
Around every 2nd or 3rd week of each month, the club would also hold movie nights or socials for anyone to come.
You can join the club's Discord server to get updates on when and where the club's events are happening.

Due to the Covid pandemic, the club meetings are held online in the club's discord server.
Physical meetings may return when the University deems it safe for a large gathering of students and if the members of club wish to return to physical meetings.



Brief Overview of the Site

Club's Activites

JAMS Online Presence

KiraKiraCon
This is the club's annual 1 day convention runned by the club members and held in the UNCC Student Union.
There is a separate website made for this event, thus this page is more dedicated in explaining the history and work that goes into managing a con.

Member
The club is open to anyone to come and stick around with no need to become a member unless they wish to vote on certain things.
This page will explain about how to become a member and how to become an officer.

A contact page where any visitor to the site can send a message to the club's email.
