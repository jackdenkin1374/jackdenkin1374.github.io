Facebook

Before the existence of Discord, the club utilize Facebook as their primary social media platform.
It is still maintained as a secondary social media in case people who joins the club prefer not to use Discord.


NinerEngage

A club page for UNCC related rules and maintence. Here you will find the club's constitution and possibly current officers.


Discord

The primary social media platform that the club uses. It is much more active and features a wide array of channels to help newcomers find new friends or participate in the club.
Here are some explanations for the various channels in the server.

Catergories Channels Types of Users
General
Here are several important channels for the server regarding rules, club annoucements and a general purpose chat.

 Rules
Important rules pertaining to the server and the club as well.

Annoucements
Any pings regarding ongoing club events or important news about the club or server.

General
Landing channel where newcomers first arrive into the server.

Member
Club members' focused chat.

 There are several types of users in this server.

General
Newcomers looking around.

Member
Due paying members of the club. They are given access to some channels that non members cannot accessed.

Discord Nep
Moderators for the server in case the officers of the club are unable to deal with any issues that may occur in the server.

Bottom Nep
The 3 or 4 officers of the club, Treasurer, Vice-President, Public Relations and Secretary.

Top Nep
The President of the club.
Campus
These channels are for any on campus related events for students to receive help with school work or learn Japanese.
As well as club related meetings, such as Cosplay Corner and the regular Wednesday meetings.

 School
General channel for school related discussions, whether its help finding classes, solutions or important events happening on campus.

Cosplay Corner
General discussion chat for Cosplay Corner Members as well as any cosplay projects from other users.

Japanese
A channel to help other people learn Japanese with live practice.
Fun
Here are various channels for users to chat about fun related topics, such as gaming, memes, anime, music and so on.

 Games
General discussion chat for games or for others to find other people to play games with.

Anime & Manga
General discussion chat for anime and manga, especially for seasonal shows.

Cons & Merch
A channel where other users showcase their wares or talk of local conventions that occurs in the Charlotte area.
Mobile Games
A small selection of channels where users can talked on and on about certain mobile games which merits its own channel.
There use to be a wide amount of channels but due to inactivity or sparse conversations, the officers has reduced the number of channels in this catergory.
Nep Noises
Voice channels for meetings, game sessions or just chatting.
Archive
Old channels where users no longer post in, but still hold some importance to the club.

 KiraKiraCon
General planning chat for any members to share details about KKC to anyone else.
